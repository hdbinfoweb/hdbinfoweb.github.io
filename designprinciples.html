Design Principles

The design of the HDB InfoWEB establishes a friendly and personable atmosphere, offering an online extension of the service citizens have become accustomed to in real life. Its design leans heavily on the HDB brand values and offers content in an easy to access manner. Its design focuses on clarity.

Baseline grids and recommended layouts are provided to improve user experience by enabling easy navigation. This guide also provides clear instructions on the choice of visual elements. In addition to fundamental layout principles, it touches upon typography, iconography, and photographic guidelines.
