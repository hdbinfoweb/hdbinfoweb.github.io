Colors

We can project a helpful, reliable, and accessible brand image with good colour usage.

  • Primary Colours

    The HDB Red is incorporated across the website design to establish a sense of familiarity with the HDB Brand. Additionally, it is used on key calls-toaction that bring the user to a new page, be that within the HDB InfoWEB itself or an external source.

    HEX: #CC0001
    RGB: 204 0 1


  • Secondary Colours

    For secondary calls-to-action that do not force a page reload, we use Teal to still capture the attention of the user, without overpowering the primary calls-toaction. An example of this would be the buttons that trigger the expansion of an accordion, displaying previously hidden content, without reloading the page.

    HEX: #0C8188
    RGB: 12 139 136
