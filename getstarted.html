Why do we need a Design System

HDB InfoWEB Design System consists of a set of standards and guidelines that lay the visual foundation for HDB InfoWEB and services in it. Its main purpose is to provide the HDB InfoWEB developers with a reference guide of best practices, tips, collections of elements, compononets and resources to ensure visual consistency across the platform.

It is important that developers follow the principles in this Design System to create consistent interaction and visual design across HDB InfoWEB. This ensures a unifying voice, which is important in portraying a strong and memorable brand that users can connect with.

How to use it

Each element, component and template comes with explanation, preview and codes to facilitate implementation. Pay attention to the design considerations and the when to use section to ensure correct application.

Roadmap

A design system is not a deliverable, it's a product. As such, HDB InfoWEB Design System is constantly being updated and developed. We will be sharing our status tracker to follow components through their lifecycle – from conception, through design, to development, and eventually production use.
